Impactful Leaders Program

It is the vision of the Mohammed Bin Rashid Center for Leadership Development to create leaders for tomorrow’s world. To achieve this vision, the Center is launching its flagship program first. This program is designed to be an avant-garde experience that challenges leaders to move outside their comfort zone. It is a journey rather than an event, which will leave the participants being more effective at the end of the program. The curriculum is based on the Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum Competency Model and is delivered by a consortium of the world’s leading providers of executive education. The modules of the program have been carefully curated and contextualized to be readily applicable to the local context.
